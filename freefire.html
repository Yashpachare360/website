Free Fire Game Information

About Free Fire

Free Fire is a popular battle royale game developed by Garena. Players are dropped onto an island where they must fight to be the last one standing.

Gameplay

Each match consists of 50 players who parachute onto an island in search of weapons and resources to eliminate opponents and survive.

Features

  • Fast-paced 10-minute matches
  • Wide variety of weapons and characters
  • Solo, duo, and squad modes
  • Regular updates and events
